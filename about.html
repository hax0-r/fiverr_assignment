About Us

For a simple and tasty experience, select from our exclusive "My Alowishus" bottled brews, or savor one of our freshly brewed coffees for a lovely and fragrant treat.

Welcome to My Alowishus

At My Alowishus, we’re passionate about crafting the perfect cup of coffee. Our beans are carefully selected and roasted to bring out the rich, bold flavors that make every sip an experience. Whether you're starting your day or taking a break, we're here to serve you the finest coffee in town.

Our cozy café offers a warm, inviting atmosphere where you can relax and enjoy a variety of specialty drinks. From classic espressos to creative concoctions, our menu has something for every coffee lover. Join us for a delicious brew and experience the comfort and joy of My Alowishus.


Our Story

My Alowishus began with a simple dream: to create a coffee shop that feels like home. We pride ourselves on our commitment to quality and community. Every day, we strive to make each visit special by offering exceptional coffee and a friendly atmosphere. Come and be part of our story.


  • Freshly roasted coffee beans.

  • Locally sourced pastries and snacks.
  • Comfortable seating and free Wi-Fi.

Specialty Brews
